# ASO Optimizer - Usage Examples

This file contains practical examples you can use to test and learn the ASO Optimizer skill.

## Quick Test Examples

Copy and paste these directly into Claude to see the skill in action.

### Example 1: Photo Editing App (Complete ASO)

```
I have a photo editing app called "PhotoMagic" for iOS. It has:
- 50+ professional filters
- Collage maker with templates
- AI background removal
- Beauty retouching tools
- Batch editing
- Export to Instagram, TikTok

Target audience: Instagram content creators, ages 18-35.
I need complete ASO help.
```

**Expected output:**
- 50+ keywords organized by tier
- Competitor analysis of top photo editing apps
- Optimized App Name, Subtitle, Keywords field
- Complete ASO report

---

### Example 2: Fitness App (Competitor Focus)

```
I'm launching a fitness tracking app for iOS and Android called "FitFlow". 
It tracks workouts, calories, and creates custom workout plans.

I want to focus on competitor analysis. Who are my main competitors 
and what keywords are they using?
```

**Expected output:**
- List of top 5-10 competitor apps
- Their keyword strategies
- Gap analysis and opportunities
- Competitive positioning insights

---

### Example 3: Budget App (Localization)

```
I have a budget tracking app "MoneyWise" currently in English.
I want to expand to Italian and Spanish markets.

Current keywords: budget, money, expense, tracker, finance, savings

Help me localize for Italy and Spain.
```

**Expected output:**
- Translated and culturally adapted keywords for IT and ES
- Local competitor research
- Localized metadata for both markets
- Comparison matrix

---

### Example 4: Productivity App (Keyword Refresh)

```
My to-do list app isn't getting downloads. Current keywords:
todo, list, task, reminder, planner

These don't seem to be working. What should I change?
```

**Expected output:**
- Analysis of current keywords
- Comparison with competitor keywords
- Specific replacement suggestions
- Prioritized action plan

## Output Format Examples

### What to Expect

#### Tier 1 Keywords (for Title/Subtitle)
```
1. photo editor (High relevance, high volume)
2. picture effects (Medium-high relevance, medium volume)
3. instagram editor (High relevance, medium volume)
```

#### iOS Metadata
```
App Name (30 chars): PhotoMagic - Photo Editor

Subtitle (30 chars): Filters, Effects & Collage

Keywords (100 chars): filter,effect,collage,beauty,retouch,instagram,edit,enhance,crop,background,ai,batch,template
```

#### Competitor Analysis
```
COMPETITOR: VSCO

Keywords identified:
- In title: "photo", "video"
- In subtitle: "editor", "effects"
- Positioning: Professional creative tool

Strengths vs our app: Established brand, large community
Weaknesses vs our app: Less user-friendly, fewer features
```

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific** about your app's features
2. **Mention your target audience** clearly
3. **Specify platforms** (iOS, Android, or both)
4. **Indicate languages** if multi-market
5. **Share current keywords** if refreshing
6. **Mention competitors** you know of
7. **Describe your unique value** proposition
